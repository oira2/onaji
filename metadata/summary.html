While Gabu and Mei's life in the Emerald Forest has finally settled down, the Sawa Sawa goat herd continues to live in fear. Under the direction of their new leader, the Baku Baku wolf pack is more dangerous than ever before. The new Elder of Sawa Sawa Mountain has a plan to make life safer and better for everyone, but it will never work unless the goats and wolves can put aside their differences and learn to get along.
